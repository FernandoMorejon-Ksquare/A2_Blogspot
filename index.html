Mérida, my city

¿What is Mérida today?

Mérida is a quiet city, that has a great quality of life. In recent times, the pandemic, has brought new opportunities in the fields related to technology and computing related technologies. Due to health requirements, and the phenomenon, "home office"; remote work opportunities open in which, professionals with a long time in the field, recent graduates and enthusiasts; are benefited by need to fill jobs to meet the needs of the emerging economy. Mérida meets the requirements to be a great city for those remote workers.

My favorite food from Mérida

Mérida is know for his reknown gastronomy which uses a lot of habanero pepper, the fruit that grows in the region's farmfields. My favorite food is "La torta de Cochinita".
